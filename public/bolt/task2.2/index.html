Meet Dr. Sarah Chen

With over 15 years of dedicated experience in family therapy, Dr. Chen brings both professional expertise and genuine compassion to every session.

Dr. Chen understands that seeking therapy takes courage. Her warm, non-judgmental approach creates a safe space where families can explore challenges, process emotions, and develop healthier patterns of communication.

Specializing in evidence-based therapeutic approaches including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Gottman Method Couples Therapy, Dr. Chen has helped hundreds of families navigate their most difficult moments and emerge stronger.

Education & Credentials

  • Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology, Stanford University
  • Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT)
  • Certified Gottman Method Couples Therapist
  • Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) Level 3 Trained
