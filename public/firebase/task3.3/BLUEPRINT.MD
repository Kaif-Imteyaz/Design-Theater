
# PhotoCommunity Blueprint


## Overview


PhotoCommunity is a platform for local photographers to showcase their work, receive feedback, and connect with clients. It balances community engagement with commercial opportunities.


## Design and Features


### Version 1.0 (Initial Build)


*   **Homepage:** A welcoming entry point with a clear value proposition.
*   **Visual Design:** Modern, clean, and visually driven, with an emphasis on showcasing photography.
    *   **Colors:** A palette of dark grays, white, and a vibrant accent color.
    *   **Typography:** A clean, sans-serif font for readability.
    *   **Layout:** A responsive layout that works on all devices.
*   **Key Sections:**
    *   **Header:** Logo and navigation.
    *   **Hero Section:** A large, inviting image with a clear call to action.
    *   **Featured Photos:** A gallery of community-voted images.
    *   **Weekly Challenge:** A section promoting the current challenge.
    *   **Meet the Photographers:** A section showcasing photographer profiles.
    *   **Services:** A section for photographers to list their services for hire.
    *   **Footer:** Links to important pages and social media.
