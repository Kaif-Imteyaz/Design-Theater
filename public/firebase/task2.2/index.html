A Safe Space for Growth and Healing

When you're facing challenges, it can be hard to know where to turn. At Harmony Family Therapy, we provide a warm, non-judgmental environment where you can feel safe, understood, and hopeful. Our goal is to empower you and your loved ones with the tools to communicate better, resolve conflicts, and find a path forward.

Our Services

Couples Counseling

Strengthen your partnership, improve communication, and rediscover your connection. We help you work through conflicts and build a more resilient relationship.

Family Mediation

Navigate difficult family dynamics with guided mediation. We facilitate productive conversations to help you resolve disputes and find common ground.

Adolescent & Teen Therapy

Support your teen through the challenges of adolescence. We provide a safe space for them to explore their feelings, develop coping skills, and build self-esteem.

Meet Dr. Sarah Chen

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Sarah Chen is a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist dedicated to helping families thrive. Her approach is compassionate, collaborative, and tailored to your unique needs. She believes that with the right support, every family has the strength to overcome challenges and create a more harmonious life.

Dr. Chen holds a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology and is a certified mediator. She is committed to creating a therapeutic environment built on trust, empathy, and respect.
