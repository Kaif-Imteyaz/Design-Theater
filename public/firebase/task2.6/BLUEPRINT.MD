# MedTech Solutions Homepage Blueprint


## Overview


This document outlines the design and development plan for the MedTech Solutions homepage. The goal is to create a visually compelling and trustworthy online presence that effectively communicates the value of our AI-powered diagnostic tools to a diverse audience of medical professionals and hospital administrators.


## Visual Strategy


The visual design aims to strike a balance between technological innovation and medical professionalism, ensuring the interface is accessible and easy to use in various hospital environments.


### Color Palette


*   **Primary:** Deep Blue (`#0A2540`) - Evokes trust, stability, and professionalism.
*   **Accent:** Teal (`#30C5A3`) - Represents innovation, technology, and positive outcomes.
*   **Neutrals:**
    *   Off-White (`#F6F9FC`) - For text and elements on dark backgrounds.
    *   Light Gray (`#E6E6E6`) - For borders and subtle UI elements.
    *   Dark Gray (`#1E1E1E`) - For backgrounds to reduce eye strain.


### Typography


*   **Headings:** "Manrope", a modern, clean sans-serif font.
*   **Body Text:** "Source Sans Pro", a highly readable and clear sans-serif font.


### Layout & Structure


*   **Clean & Professional:** A spacious, grid-based layout for a sense of order.
*   **Hero Section:** A powerful headline with a compelling, abstract background image.
*   **Features:** Icon-driven explanations of key software benefits.
*   **Data Visualization:** An intuitive "confidence bar" to explain AI scores.
*   **Credibility:** Sections for testimonials, partner logos, and research.
