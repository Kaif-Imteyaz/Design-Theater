# FreelanceFlow Blueprint


## Overview


FreelanceFlow is a marketplace connecting businesses with freelance designers, developers, writers, and marketers. The platform focuses on long-term relationships and quality matches.


## Project Outline


### Design and Style


*   **Aesthetics:** Modern, clean, and professional design.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary: #4A90E2 (Blue)
    *   Secondary: #F5A623 (Orange)
    *   Accent: #7ED321 (Green)
    *   Neutral: #4A4A4A (Dark Gray), #9B9B9B (Gray), #FFFFFF (White)
*   **Typography:**
    *   Headings: "Montserrat", sans-serif
    *   Body: "Open Sans", sans-serif
*   **Iconography:** Use modern, easily understandable icons to represent different sections and features.
*   **Layout:** Responsive and mobile-first design with a clear visual hierarchy.


### Features


*   **Homepage:**
    *   Hero section with a clear call-to-action (CTA).
    *   "How it Works" section explaining the process.
    *   "Why Choose Us" section highlighting the platform's benefits.
    *   Testimonials from clients and freelancers.
    *   Footer with navigation links and contact information.
*   **Project Management:**
    *   Project briefs with detailed requirements.
    *   Custom proposals from freelancers.
    *   Interview scheduling and communication tools.
    *   Contract generation and milestone management.
    *   Payment processing and invoicing.
*   **User Profiles:**
    *   Client profiles with project history.
    *   Freelancer profiles with portfolios and reviews.
