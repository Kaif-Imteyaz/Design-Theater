Encouraging Participation While Maintaining Quality

Problem: Reputation systems can be gamed with fake praise.
Solution: Only meaningful engagement counts‚Äîdetailed feedback, thoughtful critiques, quality comments. Quick likes don't build reputation. This keeps quality high.

Feedback Quality Standards

What We Require: Feedback must be specific ("Great use of negative space in the corner composition") not generic ("Nice photo"). Comments must offer something actionable or constructive.

Curated Challenges

How: Challenges are themed by community voting monthly. Winners are featured (traffic & visibility), not paid. The prize is prominence and feedback from serious photographers.

Moderation by Reputation

System: New members' feedback goes to queue for approval. Experienced photographers with high reputation can post directly. Scales from strict ‚Üí trusted

Mentorship Matching
