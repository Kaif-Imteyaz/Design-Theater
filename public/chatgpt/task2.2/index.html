FAQs

Is therapy right for us if we’re skeptical?

It’s normal to feel unsure. Our first call is about understanding your goals and making an honest plan. You’re in control of the pace and the process.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes—secure video sessions are available for individuals, couples, and families.

Is everything confidential?

Yes. We follow strict privacy standards. We’ll also discuss limits to confidentiality (like safety concerns) during your first session.

What are your fees?

We’ll review fees and sliding‑scale options during your consult so you can make an informed decision.
