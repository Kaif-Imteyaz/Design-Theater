FAQ

Questions we get a lot

Is this gluten‑free?

Some SKUs are. Look for the GF icon on the front and the allergen callout near the Nutrition Facts. Always read the ingredient list for your specific allergens.

Do you use soy?

We keep soy optional: several items are soy‑free (like Smash Burgers), others use non‑GMO soy for specific textures (like Korean BBQ Ribs).

How do these cook?

High heat. Pan, grill, or air‑fryer. Finish with oil + salt. Go louder than you think for best crisp.
